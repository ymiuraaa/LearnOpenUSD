# Asset Parameterization

In this lesson, you’ll learn about asset parameterization in OpenUSD, which enables the reuse of content by allowing certain fields and properties to vary downstream. Here’s what we’ll accomplish:

* Explain the purpose and benefits of asset parameterization in OpenUSD  
* Differentiate between variants and primvars as parameterization methods  
* Organize our assets
